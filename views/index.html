Online Transport Management System

Using this online transport management system we can montor and manage buses as well as traffic. Which currently on having all type of facilities is not being used properly and the citizens without participating in this will get results and their common problem will be solved.


Features of Bus Management System

Real Time Monitoring

An online transport management system will monitor bus positions using GPS like we are already using in CHALO APP. By using chalo app when we can track the position of bus then why we can not solve the problem of their being late Using this data we can contact driver and inform him the timings of bus. As well as our monitors on bus stop which tell the timing of bus we can put their real time data.

Crowd Distribution

As buses will reach on time at bus stops the crowd will be distributed in all buses and the buses will not get overloaded.

Traffic Management

Using gps monitoring and cctv footages of all traffic signals we can monitor the traffic and resolve the problem of over traffic. By diverting routes informing traffic police etc.

Maintainance

The Management System will keep the record of all the bus running on roads and have all their info and put their dates of maintainance If bus does not get fitted the bus will not run on roads.
